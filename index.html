A LIGHT ALARMING DEVICE

It's a tiny alarming device which takes effect when there is no enough light.

There are certain cases we ignore that the light around is not suitable for us to read or write. With the decive, you are informed when there is no suitable light.

The device only take effect when you turn it on.

CONTROL UR VOICE

It's a game you will win only if your voice is of the exact loudness.

What to show your ability to control your voice?

The light will inform that ur voice now is louder or lower.

(red-louder\blue-lower\green-exact)

And the clock will show u about the deviation level.
